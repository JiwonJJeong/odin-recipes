Pesto Pasta

Description

Pesto pasta is a delicious pasta recipe using the Italian pesto sauce. Pesto sauce has a creamy, herby, and slightly nutty flavor, and it is made from basil, pine nuts, garlic, cheese, and olive oil. Additions like onions, salt and pepper, and cheese taste great with this simple pasta recipe.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pesto sauce
  • Onions and olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salt water to desired consistency and drain.
  2. Cook the onions in olive oil, until the onions are translucent.
  3. Stir in the pesto and seasonings to the onions.
  4. Add the onion and pesto mix to your hot pasta. Add shredded cheese on top.
