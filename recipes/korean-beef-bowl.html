Korean Beef Bowl

Description

This Korean beef bowl recipe is easy to make and delicious with ground beef and rice as the main components. Soy, ginger, garlic, sesame seed, and crushed red pepper additions make this dish nutty and savory. This easy recipe can also be eaten with vegatables instead of rice!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 5 crushed cloves of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 6 chopped green onions
  • 4 cups cooked rice
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Heat skillet to medium-high heat. Add beef and cook for 5-7 minutes. Drain excess grease.
  2. Stir in garlic, ginger, and sesame oil. Cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in soy sauce, brown sugar, and red pepper. Cook for 7 minutes.
  4. Add half of the chopped green onions.
  5. Serve the mixture over hot cooked rice. Garnish with sesame seeds and remaining green onions.
